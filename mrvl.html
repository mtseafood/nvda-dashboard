Key risks to watch
📡
Telecom & enterprise weakness
Marvell's traditional telecom and enterprise segments are recovering slowly. If AI revenue misses, there is little else to offset the gap.
⚔️
Custom ASIC competition from Broadcom
Broadcom competes directly for the same hyperscaler custom silicon contracts. Marvell must continue winning new design slots to sustain growth.
📉
High valuation on forward estimates
At ~25x forward P/E, MRVL is priced for strong AI ASIC execution. Any guidance cut or design win loss would trigger a sharp re-rating.
🌏
Geopolitical & export risk
US export restrictions on advanced semiconductors could limit Marvell's addressable market in Asia-Pacific data center deployments.
